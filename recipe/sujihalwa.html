Suji ka Halwa

Description

Is an ethnic Indian Dessert rich in flavours and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Suji(semolina)
  • 3-4 Teaspoon full Ghee
  • 2-3 Cup Milk
  • Some Cashew, Almonds and Raisins
  • Sugar for taste

Recipe

  1. Add Ghee in pre heated pan
  2. After Ghee is melted add semolina and stir.
  3. Roast semolina till is gets the golden brown color
  4. Add the dry fruits while roasting
  5. Add sugar
  6. When every thing gets roasted add luke warm milk.
  7. boil until all the milk disappear and Nice paste kinda texture shows up

Enjoy..

Caution..

While adding milk to roasted Semolina it can have little voilent splashes.
