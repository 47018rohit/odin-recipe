Aloo ke Parathe

Description

Paratha is one of the most loved North Indian delicacy, which is prepared in almost every Indian household.

Ingredients

  • 2 potato
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander leaves
  • 2 tablespoon onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup water

Recipe

  1. Boil the potatoes at least a few hours before you plan to make the parathas, mash them in a large bowl
  2. Add chopped onions, coriander leaves, salt, garam masala powder and red chilli powder.
  3. Mix well so that no lumps remain
  4. For the Dough, Put wheat flour in a large mixing bowl
  5. Add water gradually and knead into a soft dough
  6. Make small-medium balls of the dough and roll them out into 3 to 4-inch circles.
  7. Add a spoonful of potato filling in the centre.
  8. Seal the dough and round it with your fingers.
  9. Now, roll them with a rolling pin into round parathas.
  10. Heat an iron tawa and roast the parathas
  11. Apply ghee with a kitchen brush on both sides
  12. Serve hot with curd, pickle and chutney

Enjoy..!!
