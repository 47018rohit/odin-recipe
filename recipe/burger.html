Burger

Description

The fastest way to make your gut feel good.

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Cheeze
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Pickle
  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Some spices

Recipe

  1. First marinate the chicken breast with some salt, spices and Lemon
  2. Cut the buns in Halves and light toast it with butter
  3. Shallow Fry your Chicken for about 15min. on medium heat.
  4. Now apply ketchup on the buns and add ring cut Onion and Tomato
  5. Add Chicken and add cheeze on the top of it.
  6. Now put the top of the bun. Your burger is ready.

    7. Enjoy..
